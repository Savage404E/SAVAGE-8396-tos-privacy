SAVAGE Discord Bot Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for using SAVAGE, a multi-feature Discord bot offering entertainment, economy, gambling, and social commands. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.
By using SAVAGE, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

  • Automatically Collected:
    • User ID and Server ID (for user data tracking per server)
    • Command usage statistics (e.g., to track balances, rankings, inventory)
    • Timestamps (for features like !daily, !streak)
  • User-Provided:
    • In-game activity data (e.g., !give, !trade, !buy)
    • User interactions with commands (!hug, !fight, !mine, etc.)

We do not collect or store any personal data like emails, real names, or payment information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Power bot features like economy, gambling, ranking, and card collection
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., inventory, pet ownership, card ownership)
  • Prevent abuse and ensure fair play (e.g., cooldown enforcement, anti-cheat checks)

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely using encrypted databases.
  • Access is limited to authorized developers only.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you use the bot.
  • If you leave a server or request deletion, your data will be removed upon request (see Section 7).

6. Third-Party Services

SAVAGE may use third-party services (e.g., for hosting or databases) that may process data on our behalf. These services are required to follow data protection laws.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke data processing (which may disable features)

To exercise these rights, contact the bot developer or server administrator.

8. Children's Privacy

SAVAGE is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you believe we are storing data of a minor without consent, please contact us for removal.

9. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of SAVAGE after changes means you accept the updated terms.
